When it comes to home improvement many homeowners don't know where to start. " Should I replace windows first? But what about the roof? We've always wanted a new kitchen!" The Home Improvement Planner (HIP) is a web application that assists homeowners by helping them prioritize projects based on the age of key components in their home, potential energy savings, Return on Investment and personal concerns. By answering a few simple questions homeowners will get instant information and a suggested priority list so they can make home improvement decisions with confidence.

But HIP doesn't stop there! Get access to DIY tips and advice on hiring a contractor. HIPs contractor evaluation tool helps you weigh contractors against each other and choose the best one for you. Finally, HIP will give you a project roadmap that will guide you through the entire process of executing a project. Just like your home, HIP will improve with time and add new features to increase convenience and lower stress that often accompanies a remodeling proect. I hope you can help make HIP a reality!

FAQ

What qualifies you to make this app?
With over 5 years in the home improvement industry and a technical degree I'm thoroughly familiar with the major components of a house and remodeling practices. I've done the DIY thing, worked for professional contractors, sparred with insurance agents, and researched industry metrics. I know the major things to consider when starting a project and know which resources to consult when evaluating remodeling decisions. I'm also constantly consulting other industry professionals to keep up with modern trends.
What platforms will this app be available for?
The app will be platform agnostic and accessible via a web browser. Therefore whether you're on an Iphone, Android, or Windows phone you will have full access. Additionally, you will be able to use the app in a standard desktop browser.
